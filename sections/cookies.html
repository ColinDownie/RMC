How we use cookies and similar technologies on this website.

Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how the Ross Consultancy Group (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (“the Site”). By continuing to use the Site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the Site function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical insights.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function. They enable core features such as navigation and security.

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the Site, allowing us to improve content, navigation, and user experience. We may use trusted third‑party analytics tools for this purpose.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences (such as language or region) to enhance your experience.

4. Third‑Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third‑party services embedded on the Site, such as analytics or embedded media. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • ensure the Site functions correctly,
  • improve performance and usability,
  • analyse traffic and usage patterns,
  • and enhance the overall user experience.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the Site.

Instructions for managing cookies can typically be found in your browser’s help section.
